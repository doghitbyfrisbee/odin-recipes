How to cook a cup of Ramen for brokes mfs

A cup of ramen can feed a man for a whole day

for only 39 cents plus seasoning if you crazy like that

ingredients

  • Ramen packet
  • Water
  • Chicken broth (optional)
  • Salt Pepper (optional)
  • Bowl (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil water or chicken broth
  2. Once water is hot put the dry noodles into hot water/broth
  3. Use flavor packet if you used water if you used broth keep packet for furture use
  4. Salt and pepper at this point and add other spices (personally I like hot peppers in it)
  5. Let cool and enjoy
